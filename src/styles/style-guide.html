Sing for Hope Classroom Style Guide
Introduction
The Sing for Hope Classroom website is meant to feel like an extension of the Sing for Hope brand. Core brand elements, like the logo and brand green, are used as foundational elements, and the Global Goals color palette and iconography is layered on top of it. Additional pieces, such as the typeface, rounded corners, and building blocks element, have been added to add approachability and fun to the site. The sections below introduce all of the different site elements. Refer to the website components and layouts for a more in depth view of how everything comes together.
Logo Lockup
Our logo lockup pairs the Sing For Hope logo and the word 'Classroom,' set in Founders Grotesk. The lockup has two color variations as specified below.
Color
Primary Palette
We use a minimal primary palette, consisting of Sing For Hope Green and neutrals. This creates a simple foundation for the use of imagery and the 17 colors in the Sustainable Development Goals palette.
Typography
Founders Grotesk
Founders Grotesk is used for both headlines and body copy throughout the site. The round, geometric forms and playful descenders add character and approachability to the site. On some of the smaller header sizes, the weight jumps from Regular to Medium, this ensures they can be used to add hierarchy in more text-heavy sections of the site.
